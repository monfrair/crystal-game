The Crystal Collectors Game

You are be given a random number at the beginning of the game.
There are pictures of four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your total score is higher than the random number.
The values of the crystals change for each game.
