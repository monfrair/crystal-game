The Crystal Collectors Game

You are given a random number at the beginning of the game.
There are pictures of four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add points to your total score.
You win by matching your score and the random number.
You lose if your total score is higher than the random number.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Crystal Skulls with a random number assinged to each, click on them to add to your total.
